Course content

List of the course content.

  • About us, story, community.
  • Introduction to open science (What is open science? Definitions and areas it includes.)
  • Open research and open data (Definitions, legislation. Examples, including life science and sociology.)
  • Pros and cons
  • Research data lifecycle (Phases or research data lifecycle. Data management. Data management plan. Phases when open data and open science get important.)
  • FAIR principles (The concept of FAIR data introduced.)
  • Data centers and data repositories (The process. Examples for different types of data.)
  • Policies (Data ownership, management, and sharing.)
  • Data ethics (Consideration of ethical implications in different areas of science. GDPR.)
  • Coding and other skills (Importance of these skills for open data. Examples.)
  • Communication and ethics of open science (Sharing, learning, teaching.)
  • Opening your research (Tools, tips, examples.)
